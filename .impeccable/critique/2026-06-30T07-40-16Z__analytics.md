## Priority Issues

[P1] Efficiency metrics have no visible definition — formula exists only in aria-label; sighted users have no tooltip or inline explanation.

[P1] Error states have no recovery action — ErrorState renders message only, no retry button. Full page reload required on API failure.

[P2] No date range presets — manual ISO date entry for every reporting cycle; no validation for inverted from/to ranges.

[P2] "Overdue/Rejected" column merges two distinct failure signals — scheduling failure vs quality failure require different management responses.

[P2] No drill-through from aggregate counts to source records — BarList items and chart bars are not linked; investigation workflows impossible.
